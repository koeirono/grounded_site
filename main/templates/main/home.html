Where Plant Care meets Self Care

At Grounded, we believe that tending to plants is more than a hobby - it’s a form of healing. In caring for something living, we slow down, reconnect, and make space for our own growth. Whether you're repotting a new leaf or pausing for a matcha in our café, every experience here is designed to help you return to yourself.

This isn’t just a plant shop. It’s a place to root, restore, and reimagine what wellness can look like—one breath, one bloom, one moment at a time.
